Sofia Pace is a multidisciplinary performance artist whose work explores the inner self through the body and its material conditions. Drawing from her Latin American upbringing and improvisational movement practices, she engages ephemerality, embodied memory, nostalgia, and found archives as sites of both inquiry and celebration. Working across dance, web-based media, and textile forms, her practice examines how corporeality is shaped, distorted, and performed, rendering the body abstract in shape but real in motion.
